Instructions & Notes

  • Sale Price: Enter the expected or agreed-upon sale price of your home
  • Commission Rates: Traditional rates typically range from 5-6%, while low-fee services often charge 1.5-2.5%
  • Transfer Tax: Varies by state/locality. Common rates are 0.1% to 1.5% of sale price
  • Title & Settlement: These are typical flat fees that vary by region and property value
  • HOA Fees: Include any condo/HOA-related costs like resale packages, prorations, and move-out fees
  • Preparation Costs: Include staging, repairs, cleaning, photography, and other pre-listing expenses
  • Credits & Concessions: Any buyer credits, repair allowances, or closing cost assistance
  • All calculations are estimates. Actual costs may vary based on your specific situation and local market conditions
